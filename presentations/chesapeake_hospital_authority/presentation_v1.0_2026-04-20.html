1. Entity Overview

Chesapeake Hospital Authority is a political subdivision of the Commonwealth of Virginia operating as a public healthcare system in Chesapeake, VA. The Authority oversees a multi-entity structure:

EntityRoleKey Detail
Chesapeake General Hospital (CGH)Primary acute-care hospital~87% of consolidated patient revenue ($442M FY2025)
Chesapeake Hospital AuthorityParent governance bodyCharges CGH 2% management fee (~$7.4M)
CHA FoundationInvestment holding entity$231–261M endowment; $29.1M investment return (FY2025)
Tidewater Orthopaedics (TOBH)Equity-method investeeOwnership 75% → 92%; $32–41M investment
SouthCare Clinic (SCC)Subsidiary clinic83% Authority ownership (FY2021)
Structural note: The Foundation holds $261M in investments generating $29.1M in returns (FY2025), but these returns are not available for hospital operating cash needs due to entity separation. This is a key liquidity consideration.
