Margin Compression Trajectory (FY2020–FY2025)

The core problem: Revenue grew $163M (+45%) but operating income fell $12M (−27%) from FY2021 peak. Expenses grew faster than revenue in 4 of 5 years. At current trajectory, CHA reaches breakeven by FY2027.
