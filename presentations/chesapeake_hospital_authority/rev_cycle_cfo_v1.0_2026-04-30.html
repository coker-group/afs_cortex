Root Cause Drivers

  • 1Payer yield erosion: Anthem (highest reimbursement) down 26%→21% of mix; −$25M in foregone premium revenue
  • 2Self-pay growth unchecked: 2%→4% of AR; est. $14M uncollectible at 30% yield rate
  • 3Collection capacity gap: Revenue +45% over 5 yrs; AR staff/systems not scaled — FY2025 AR grew $9M in single year
  • 4Denial volatility: 3rd party liability spiked 3.3x to $12.2M in FY2023; pattern suggests coding/auth gaps
  • 5Operating CF collapse: $9.4M→$1.7M despite $158M revenue growth; cash not flowing from operations
Bottom line: CHA is running a $509M revenue engine that converts only $1.7M to operating cash. The rev cycle is the primary correctable lever — every 1 day of AR improvement frees $1.4M in working capital.
