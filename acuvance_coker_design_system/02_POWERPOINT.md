## Color Blocking Style

Acuvance Coker presentations use **bold geometric color blocking** as a signature visual style:

- Large solid Vibrant Blue (`#004BFF`) rectangles, panels, or circles occupying significant slide area
- Geometric shapes (rectangles, squares) as accent elements at edges and corners
- Photography integrated within or adjacent to color blocks
- The stepping-rectangle motif (three offset rectangles) bridges color-block boundaries

### Color Block Rules

| Element | Color | Text Color |
|---|---|---|
| Primary panel (cover slide left half) | `#004BFF` | White |
| Dark stat callout boxes | `#22272D` | White |
| Section divider circle | `#004BFF` | White (logo inside) |
| Accent stepping rectangles | `#004BFF`, `#0C36B4`, `#22272D` | — |
| Content slide background | `#FFFFFF` | `#22272D` (body), `#004BFF` (titles) |

## Slide Type Specifications

### Cover / Title Slide

- **Left half:** Full Vibrant Blue (`#004BFF`) background
  - White Acuvance Coker logo at bottom-left
  - Dark label/tag at top-left (e.g., "Introduction")
  - Title text in white, subtitle/year below
- **Right half:** Professional photography (color, healthcare/business setting)
  - Blue rectangle overlay in lower-right area
  - Geometric stepping rectangles bridging the blue and photo areas
- **Logo:** White reverse variant, bottom-left of blue panel

### Section Divider Slides

**Style A — Photo Circle:**
- Full-bleed grayscale (B&W) photograph background
- Large Vibrant Blue circle centered on slide
- White Acuvance Coker logo inside the circle
- Dark label/tag at top of circle

**Style B — Split Panel:**
- White left panel with section title
- Photo top-right quadrant
- Solid Vibrant Blue rectangle bottom-right quadrant

### Content Slides

- **Background:** White
- **Title area:** Thin Vibrant Blue horizontal rule spanning full width near top; slide title in `#004BFF`, Arial, title case, positioned just below
- **Body:** Black/charcoal Arial text
- **Footer:**
  - Bottom-left: Acuvance Coker logo (full color)
  - Bottom-center: `www.cokergroup.com`
  - Bottom-right: Page number (`pg. X`)
- **Copyright:** `© [Year] Acuvance Coker. All rights reserved. No reproduction without permission.`

### Contact / Closing Slide

- Left side: Contact information block (name, title, email, office locations with map pin icons)
- Right side: Map visualization or branded graphic
- Full Vibrant Blue background for header area
- White logo placement

## Table Formatting

### Column Headers

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Fill | `#004BFF` (Vibrant Blue) |
| Text color | White |
| Font | Arial, 10pt, Bold |
| Alignment | Centered |

| Row Type | Fill | Text Color | Font |
|---|---|---|---|

| Grand Total | `#004BFF` | White | Arial 10pt Bold |
| Footnote | `#F3F4F5` (Light Gray) | Black | Arial 9pt Italic |

### Table Borders

- Outer border: `#0C36B4` (Deep Blue), 1pt
- Internal horizontal rules: `#0C36B4`, 0.5pt
- No vertical internal borders (clean horizontal-rule style preferred)

## Icon Usage

### Style

Acuvance Coker uses **Microsoft Office Fluent icons** in two treatments:

1. **Outline icons** — on light backgrounds, colored in `#004BFF` or `#0C36B4`
2. **Solid fill icons** — on colored/dark backgrounds, in white

### Framing

Icons are typically placed inside **colored circles**:
- Vibrant Blue circle with white icon (most common)
- White circle with blue icon (on dark backgrounds)
- Circle diameter: 48–64px (0.5–0.75") depending on slide context

### Placement Patterns

- **Service category headers:** Icon in blue circle above or left of category name
- **Feature lists:** Small icon circles left-aligned with descriptive text to the right
- **Stat callouts:** Icon inside dark callout boxes next to large numbers
- **Process/timeline flows:** Icons as step indicators

## Stat / Metric Call-Outs

Highlight key statistics using **dark charcoal boxes**:

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Background | `#22272D` (Brand Charcoal) |
| Text | White, Arial |
| Number size | 28–36pt Bold |
| Label size | 10–12pt Regular |
| Icon | White outline, small (24px) |
| Corner radius | 4–8px |

Example: `35+ Years of Experience` — large white "35+" with small "Years of Experience" label below, icon to the left.

## Photography in PPTX

- Use professional healthcare/business photography
- Mix full-color and B&W within the same deck
- B&W for section divider backgrounds, color for cover and feature slides
- Never use photography as backgrounds on content/data slides
- All photos must have high resolution (minimum 150 DPI at placed size)
- Apply the blue circle or blue panel overlay when placing logos on photography

## Template-Specific Notes for AI Generation

When generating PPTX files programmatically:

1. **Always use the template** (`2026_PPT_Acuvance Coker_Template.potx`) as the base — never create from scratch without the template
2. **Logo is an image** — embed the PNG/SVG logo asset, never render as text
3. **Grid Shapes** appear as grouped rectangle shapes in the template — preserve them, do not delete or restyle
4. **Stepping rectangles** are part of the cover slide layout — do not remove or reposition
5. **Icons** should use the Microsoft Office icon library embedded in the template
6. **Alt text** is required on all images, icons, and charts for accessibility
7. **Slide numbering** uses the format `pg. X` in the bottom-right footer
